Welcome to VelvetUI

Introducing VelvetUI, your go-to CSS component library for adding a touch of elegance and style to your web projects. It's like a toolkit of design elements that make your website look polished and feel smooth. From buttons to forms, VelvetUI provides easy-to-use components that bring a modern and classy vibe to your interface. Whether you're building a business site or a cool app, VelvetUI helps you create a sleek and consistent look across different devices. Elevate your web design effortlessly with VelvetUI's simple yet sophisticated style.
Some Key Characteristics of VelvetUI effectively:
  • Stylish Components: Ready-to-use, fashionable design elements for buttons and forms.
  • Consistent Look: Creates a polished, uniform design for a cohesive appearance across your website or app.
  • Easy Integration: Simple for developers to seamlessly incorporate VelvetUI components into their projects.
  • Adaptable to Devices: Ensures a smooth user experience by adjusting components seamlessly to different screen sizes.
  • Modern and Classy: Aligns with contemporary design trends, effortlessly adding an elegant touch to your web interfaces.

Avatar

An avatar, resembling a digital portrait, serves as a friendly profile picture, adding a personal touch whether displayed on a user's profile page or within the navigation bar for quick identification.
Choose from various sizes for your avatars by using the "avatar-container" class. Easily customize the size with classes like "avatar-large", "avatar-medium" or "avatar-small". Simply include the desired size class along with the main container class, such as "avatar-container avatar-medium" and use any image for your avatar.

Buttons

VelvetUI's buttons are designed for simplicity and style. Whether you need a primary call-to-action or a subtle secondary button, our collection offers a clean and modern buttons. Easy to integrate and customize, VelvetUI buttons effortlessly elevate the visual appeal of your website or application, ensuring a polished and user-friendly experience.
We have two categories of buttons:
  1. Solid Buttons
  2. Outlined Buttons
Solid Buttons
VelvetUI's solid buttons offer a straightforward and sleek design for your web elements. Easily distinguishable by classes like btn-primary or btn-warning, these buttons provide a clean and vibrant look for various actions. Whether signaling success, danger, or a primary call-to-action, VelvetUI's solid buttons are effortlessly integrated into your project with a simple class application. Elevate your user interface with these polished and visually appealing buttons, making interactions both intuitive and stylish.
Outlined Buttons
VelvetUI's outlined buttons offer a sleek and unobtrusive design. With a subtle border and a choice of colors, these buttons provide a clean and modern look to your web elements. Whether it's a primary call-to-action or a secondary option, VelvetUI's outlined buttons maintain a simple yet stylish appearance, seamlessly integrating into your website or application for a polished user experience.
